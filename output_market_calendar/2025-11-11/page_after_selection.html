Globex Trading Hours and Holiday Schedules

Please note that with the 2026 calendar year the CME Group will be providing downloadable holiday calendars for Globex and incorporating with Regular Trading hours into one location. To view the trading hours for a specific holiday please select the appropriate date in the “DATE” field and the data will automatically update in the table below the date field. You can then filter for the various products that you need to view. To view the trading hours for the next upcoming holiday you can click on the “View trading hours for (holiday name)” link that appears to the right of the date field.

PREOPEN

Order Entry, modification, and cancel are allowed. No order matching.

OPEN

Start of continuous trading phase. Order matching begins.

Paused

Interruption of continuous trading. Only order cancellation is allowed. No order matching.

pcp (POST CLOSE - PREOPEN)

Allows GTC/GTD orders only placement, modification, and cancellation. No order matching.

closed

Final Close of the date. Day and GTD (current trade date) orders are eliminated.

Holiday Notes

2026 Juneteenth Notes

  • Any day orders entered after the pre-open session on Thursday, June 18, will automatically cancel at the close of trading on Friday, June 19.
  • June 18th, TAM, TAM (Platts) & TAS: Regular close per each product schedule

2026 Independence Day Notes

  • Any day orders entered after the pre-open session on Thursday, July 2, will automatically cancel at the close of trading on Friday, July 3.
  • July 2nd, TAM, TAM (Platts) & TAS: Regular close per each product schedule

2026 Labor Day Notes

  • Day orders entered after a product’s Pre-opening(s) on Sunday, Septemper 6th are for trade date Tuesday, September 8th and can continue working until their Tuesday, September 8th Globex close.
  • September 4th, TAM, TAM (Platts) & TAS: Regular close per each product schedule
  • Tuesday, September 8th: Regular CME Group Globex close for all products

2026 Thanksgiving Day Notes

  • Day orders entered after a product’s Pre-opening(s) on Wednesday, November 25th are for trade date Friday, November 27th and can continue working until their Friday, November 27th Globex close.
  • November 25th: TAM, TAM (Platts) & TAS: regular close per each product schedule
  • November 27th: Regular closes for Metals TAS contracts
  • Crude, Heating Oil, RBOB & Nat Gas TAS contracts Regular close
  • Grain, Oilseed & Livestock TAS close early at 12:05 CT
  • Bitcoin TAS Early close 12:00 CT

2026 Christmas Day Notes

  • Day orders entered after a product’s Pre-opening(s) on Wednesday, December 23th, are for trade date Thursday December 24th and can continue working until their Thursday , December 24th Globex close.
  • December 23th : TAS: early close per product schedule
    • Copper TAS: Early close 11:00 CT / 12:00 ET
    • Silver TAS: Early close 11:25 CT / 12:25 ET
    • Gold TAS: Early close 11:30 CT / 12:30 ET
    • Platinum TAS: Early close 11:05 CT / 12:05 ET
    • Palladium TAS: Early close 11:00 CT / 12:00 ET
    • Crude, Heating Oil, RBOB & Nat Gas TAS: Early close at 12:30 CT / 13:30 ET
    • Grain, Oilseed & Livestock TAS close early at 12:00 CT
    • Bitcoin TAS: Early close 12:00 CT

2027 Near Year's Day Notes

  • Day orders entered after a product’s Pre-opening(s) on Wednesday, December 30th are for trade date Thursday December 31st and can continue working until their Thursday December 31st Globex close.
  • December 31 TAS: Regular close per each product schedule

2027 Martin Luther King Day Notes

  • Day orders entered after a product’s Pre-opening(s) on Sunday, January 17th, are for trade date Tuesday, January 19th and can continue working until their Tuesday, January 19th Globex close.
  • January 15th , TAM, TAM (Platts) & TAS: Regular close per each product schedule.
  • Tuesday, January 19th : Regular CME Group Globex close for all products.

2027 President's Day Notes

  • Day orders entered after a product’s Pre-opening(s) on Sunday, February 14th are for trade date Tuesday, February 16th and can continue working until their Tuesday, February 16th Globex close.
  • February 12th, TAM, TAM (Platts) & TAS: Regular close per each product schedule.
  • Tuesday, February 16th : Regular CME Group Globex close for all products.

2027 Good Friday Notes

  • Thursday March 25th  all markets have a regular close. 
  • No trading for Friday March 26th trade date in observence of Good Friday.

2026 CME Globex Messaging Efficiency Program Holiday Calendar

Due to various U.S. Holidays, CME Globex Messaging Efficiency Program Ratios for all Product Groups included in the Program will not be calculated for the trade dates listed below.

Please note the Excessive Messaging Threshold (EMT) and Mass Quoting (MQ) are part of the Messaging Efficiency Program but do not recognize the MEP holiday calendar. 

For example: 

  • Presidents’ Day is recognized within the MEP holiday calendar on Friday, Sunday and Monday.
  • Within EMT & MQ, Presidents’ Day would not be recognized as a holiday.
  • Friday would be a normal EMT & MQ calculation. MQ is calculated during Regular Trading Hours (RTH) only.
  • Sunday restart until the early halt on Monday, would be another EMT calculation. MQ is calculated during Regular Trading Hours (RTH) only.
  • Monday night restart through the close on Tuesday, would be yet another EMT calculation. MQ is calculated during Regular Trading Hours (RTH) only.
